Our Story

How we evolved into the trusted name in property legal & tax solutions

Founded in 2015

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

Sateesh Kumar Gullapalli with over 10 years of experience in property Legal Services, began as a small consultant providing legal and Tax advice to property owners quickly evolved as Sateesh identified a significant gap in the market – there was no single service provider offering comprehensive legal and tax solutions for property transactions. Recognizing the challenges faced by both resident Indians and NRIs in navigating the complex property landscape in India, Sateesh expanded the team to include Tax experts, Legal experts, property consultants, and documentation specialists. This multidisciplinary approach allowed Vakeel Properties to offer end-to-end solutions under one roof. The organisation witnessed rapid growth between 2018-2020 as word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied clients helped establish our reputation for professionalism, transparency, and expertise. Our focus on NRI clients particularly resonated in the market, as we helped hundreds of non-resident Indians manage their property assets in India remotely. Today, Vakeel Properties has grown into a team of 15+ professionals serving clients across India and from over 15 countries worldwide. While we've expanded significantly, our core values remain unchanged – providing hassle-free, transparent, and expert property solutions that truly serve our clients' best interests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting and working with us

What should I bring to my first consultation?

For the most productive first consultation, we recommend bringing relevant property documents (purchase deed, sale agreement, tax receipts), identification proof, and any correspondence related to your query. For online consultations, having these documents in digital format is helpful. This allows our experts to provide more specific guidance based on your situation.

How quickly will I receive a response after contacting you?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly. After submitting a contact form or booking a consultation, you'll receive an automated confirmation email immediately, followed by a personal response from our team.

Can NRIs schedule consultations outside Indian business hours?

Yes, we understand the time zone challenges for our NRI clients. We offer special appointment slots outside our regular business hours to accommodate clients in different time zones. Please mention your time zone and preferred time when booking, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request. We regularly conduct consultations with clients in the US, UK, Australia, and Middle East.

Do you offer services across all cities in India?

Yes, we provide property legal and tax services across all major cities in India. While our head office is in Bangalore, we have partner networks in Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, and Kolkata. For properties in other cities, we work with local associates to ensure proper handling of region-specific requirements. Our tax and NRI services are available nationwide irrespective of property location.
